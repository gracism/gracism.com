Gracism™
Thought for the day: Happiness creates happiness. The more happiness you have, the more you can share.

Enviroment

Gracism comes from building an environment where you can flourish. It's not about perfectionism, it's about building an environment where the small good things can grow into big good things. Gracism means attention to the fundamentals of life, and trusting that the life built on solid fundaments will be graceful.

Acceptance

Once you have built a place where you can flourish, then accept that the space will not be perfect, and know that you must flow with what the world creates around you.
